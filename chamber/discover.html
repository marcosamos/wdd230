Canaco Arch

This is an important monument in Canaco, it reminds us of the division that exists between Canaco and the United States, a large door that rises more than 30 meters and reminds us of where we come from and that we welcome all travelers

Vergel pool

Vergel pools is one of the most visited tourist places in Canaco in the summer months, it is a place to relax with the family and have fun with its more than 10 slides and more than 4 pools for all ages.

Arts Center

The Canaco arts center, in the form of a spherical building, houses different places where artists can make their presentations of all kinds. The Canaco arts center has a cinema where films inspired by art and documentaries from around the world are shown, without forgetting that during the week there are different activities for people of all ages.

Canaco Principal Street

Revolucion Avenue is the main street of Canaco and is very visited by tourists from all over the world, in it there is a wide variety of restaurants, bars and nightclubs to dance and have fun.

Canaco Stadium

The incredible soccer stadium of Canaco is internationally recognized for its amazing construction where it can be filled by more than 40,000 soccer fans. It also works as a presentation center for different international shows.

Morelos Park

The beautiful Morelos Park, known throughout Canaco for being a place that fills up with families every day ready to spend the afternoon in the different areas of the park. Moreles Park has a small zoo and aviary, it also has several areas for children to play and has an area of mechanical games. a good place for picnics.

Friendship Park

Friendship Park is well known for having a lake where birds spend their time, it is a nice place to rest under the shade of the trees and spend a nice afternoon reading a book and enjoying with acquaintances.

Canaco Beatch

Canaco beach represented by the dolphin monument is visited every year by thousands of tourists looking to spend a pleasant and fun time.

Spinning top building

The spinning top building known in Canaco as a cultural center where they have cultural and technological presentations in which all people are welcome to have fun and get excited with the different presentations.
